ABOUT US

OUR STORY

As researchers, we know the work required to produce effective results. Much of this boils down to how efficient and productive we are as a team. We have been doing things the traditional way for a long time, then it hit us, “isn’t there a way we can do things easier and in a more streamlined way?” That’s how the idea for “Pintereach” was born.

We created this tool to help us track research, save these results, and enable us collaborate better as a team, no matter the location.

And the best part of all, it’s 100% open source, allowing you and others to contribute product updates to the team.

Pintereach was developed by six students currently attending Lambda School, a remote program designed to prepare aspiring web developers for a career in the tech industry
